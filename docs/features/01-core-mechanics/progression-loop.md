# Progression Loop

The Core Loop follows a daily cycle structure.

## The Loop

1. **Morning (Care)**
   - Check Pokemon status.
   - Feed and Groom to prepare for the day.

2. **Day (Activity)**
   - **Explore**: Travel to different towns (World System).
   - **Train**: Play Porygon Lab or Math mini-games to boost stats.
   - **Battle**: Challenge NPC Trainers or Wild Pokemon.

3. **Rewards**
   - Earn **Coins** from battles/mini-games.
   - Buy **Items** (Food, Potions) at the Mart.
   - **Gacha**: Spend Coins to summon new Pokemon.

## End Goal

- Complete the Pokedex.
- Beat the Regional Champion (Tournament System).
